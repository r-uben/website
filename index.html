About me

I am Rubén Fernández-Fuertes, a 3rd year PhD student in Finance at Bocconi University (Milan, Italy). I hold a BSc degree in Mathematics from the Autonomous University of Madrid (2015-2019), an MSc in Teacher Training (2019-2020) from the European University, and an MSc in Mathematical Finance from the University of Manchester (2020-2021). On this website you can find more information about my career. Briefly speaking, I am interested in Macro-Finance, Asset Pricing, Climate Finance, Mathematical Finance and Portfolio Theory. I'm currently working on my paper titled Monetary Policy Amidst and Post-COVID: Comparing the Fed and the ECB.
